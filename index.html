The Winter 2018 Outreach Program Collage Explorations | Basic HTML With Guest Partner Ava’s Smile

For the 2018 program, we explored both the possibilities and limitations of translating poetry, collage and book art into engaging web experiences. Students discovered what is gained, what is lost, and what remains the same when taking artwork into the digital world.

For most, this was their first time expressing themselves in code. During the class we defined what a digital space is, how to structure it by creating sitemaps and how to distort it through often mischievous user flows. Students mastered the foundations of web development through HTML and CSS, which they used to code the portfolio sites you see on your left.

At the end of the five weeks, students created 4 works of book art and collage and a website with two projects which was on view in Cooper Union’s public student exhibition.
